Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup made primarily of miso paste, dashi (broth), and additional ingredients such as vegetables, seaweed, and tofu.

Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Boil 300ml of water
  2. Chop the tofu in cubicles and add it to the water
  3. Add some seaweed
  4. Add one table-spoon of miso paste
  5. Turn off the heat and wait 5 minutes
  6. Voilla. Your miso soup is ready
